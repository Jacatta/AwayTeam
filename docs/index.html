Away Team Summary

Act as captain of a star ship and crew. Travel to planets and deploy teams for resource recovery.

Orbit Scene Requirements

The Orbit Scene serves several crucial functions:
-Display command crew roster
-Display selected Crewmen
-Display deploying team sizes
-Display planet details
-Display planets deploy locations
